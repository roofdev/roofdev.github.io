A BRIEF BIO

Hello. I am Roof, an indie game developer. I try my hardest to make new, fun, and original ideas. On my YouTube Channel, I post coding related videos, ranging from dev logs to random side projects. In my spare time, I enjoy playing piano, violin, chess, videogames, and chatting with friends.

CURRENT PROJECTS

I am currently working on a game nicknamed Weapons. The main idea behind it is you have to get through a platforming game where your weapon is your character. You can pick between a bow, sword, axe, and shield and swap weapons during the game. Although development is slow at the moment because of school, I expect the game could be out by December 2021.

TOOLS I USE

I mainly program in Python and C#, although I do like to occasionally use other languages such as HTML and CSS, which are the main languages powering this site. To code, I use a mix of VSCode for non-game related work and Visual Studio and Unity. I also use Aseprite and Photoshop for most graphics. To edit my videos, I use Premiere Pro.
